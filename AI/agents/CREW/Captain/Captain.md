# Captain

A sea captain (also known as ship's captain, master, or shipmaster) is the highest authority on a merchant vessel. The captain is responsible for the safe and efficient operation of the ship, including its seaworthiness, safety and security, cargo operations, navigation, crew management, and legal compliance. Key duties include:

- Ensuring compliance with local and international laws and company policies
- Safe navigation and operation of the ship
- Management of all personnel and crew
- Handling cargo and inventory
- Maintaining ship's certificates and documentation
- Overseeing security plans and responding to threats
- Acting as a liaison with local authorities in case of incidents
- Providing medical care and coordinating with onshore personnel if needed

The captain is ultimately responsible for all persons and cargo on board and is the final authority in all matters concerning the ship.
