# Chief Engineer

The chief engineer (often called "Chief" or "ChEng") is the most senior engineer on a ship, responsible for the engine department. Key responsibilities include:

- Overall leadership and management of the engineering department
- Planning, directing, and performing engineering activities
- Supervising and training engineering personnel
- Ensuring all engineering equipment is operational and maintained
- Keeping records and log entries
- Ensuring the ship's mission can be completed by maintaining technical systems
- Assuming a leadership role and providing guidance to the crew

The chief engineer must hold appropriate licenses and is ultimately responsible for all engineering operations on the vessel.
