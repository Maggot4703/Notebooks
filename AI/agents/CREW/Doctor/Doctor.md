# Doctor

A ship's doctor (or medical officer) is responsible for the health and medical care of all crew and passengers aboard a vessel. Typical responsibilities include:

- Providing medical treatment and first aid for injuries and illnesses
- Managing medical supplies and equipment
- Maintaining health records and documentation
- Advising the captain on medical matters, including quarantine and sanitation
- Coordinating with onshore medical personnel for emergencies or evacuations

On larger ships, the doctor may be a licensed physician, while on smaller vessels, medical duties may be handled by a crew member with medical training.
