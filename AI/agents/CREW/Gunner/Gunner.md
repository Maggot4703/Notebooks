# Gunner

A gunner is a military role or rank associated with the operation of crew-served weapons, such as artillery, machine guns, or weapons on military vehicles and aircraft. Key responsibilities include:

- Operating and maintaining weapons systems
- Targeting and firing artillery or machine guns
- Serving as a member of a gun crew
- In some contexts, holding a rank equivalent to private in artillery corps
- In aircrews, operating defensive weapons (air gunner)

Gunners are essential in both land and naval military operations, providing firepower and defense.
