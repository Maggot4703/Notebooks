# Navigator

A navigator is responsible for determining and monitoring the position of a ship or aircraft, planning the journey, and ensuring safe passage. Key responsibilities include:

- Planning the route and passage
- Advising the captain or commander on timing and hazards
- Maintaining nautical charts, publications, and navigational equipment
- Monitoring weather, tides, and currents
- Using traditional and electronic navigation methods
- Ensuring all navigation data is up to date

In modern times, electronic navigation systems have reduced manual navigation tasks, but professional navigators remain proficient in traditional methods.
