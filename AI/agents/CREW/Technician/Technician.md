# Technician

A technician is a skilled worker in a field of technology, proficient in relevant skills and techniques, with a practical understanding of theoretical principles. Technicians may specialize in areas such as engineering, electronics, laboratory work, or machinery. Key responsibilities include:

- Applying technical knowledge to solve problems
- Operating, maintaining, and repairing equipment
- Following safety and quality standards
- Supporting engineers and scientists
- Specializing in fields such as systems, data, electronics, or laboratory work

Technicians are essential in research, development, industry, and business, often requiring technical education and hands-on experience.
