# Trader

A trader is a person or entity engaged in the buying and selling of goods, services, or financial instruments. Types of traders include:

- Merchant: Buys wholesale and sells at a profit
- Owner of a trading post: Exchanges goods with local populations
- Financial trader: Buys and sells stocks, bonds, derivatives, etc.
- Operator of a merchant vessel: Transports cargo or passengers for hire

Traders play a key role in commerce, finance, and the movement of goods and services.
